To use a google font, uncomment the link and paste within the HEAD tags in your HTML and use the style recommended under the link in your CSS element

Examples on hope to use are in the snippet-examples.html file, lines 21 and 27 or...

  1. Copy the 1st line of the link tag and paste in between the <head></head> tags.
  2. And copy the 2ns line of the font-family and paste in the CSS element you want it to display in.
