Where to find more information

There is a great community of Ultimate players in Halifax. You can check out the Halifax Ultimate site.

Nova Scotia has a disc sports site where you can learn where to play Ultimate near you or play other disc sports like disc golf. Check out their site.

You can even watch real professional ultimate disc players on YouTube or follow all the action of the American Ultimate Disc League. There are four Canadian teams in the league Montreal, Ottawa, Toronto and British Columbia.
